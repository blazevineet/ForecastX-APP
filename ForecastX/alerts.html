Seasonal Weather Patterns

Chennai: Cyclone Season

Chennai and the Tamil Nadu coast are vulnerable to cyclonic storms during the Northeast Monsoon season. Be extremely cautious if traveling during this period.

Typical Period: October - December

Delhi: High Pollution (Smog)

Delhi frequently experiences severe air pollution and smog post-monsoon, peaking after Diwali. This can cause respiratory issues. Consider using air purifiers and N95 masks.

Typical Period: Late October - January

Mumbai: Monsoon Flooding

The Southwest Monsoon brings heavy rainfall, often leading to waterlogging in low-lying areas and disruption of train services. Plan travel accordingly.

Typical Period: June - September

Typical Period: May - June

Himalayas: Landslide Risk

During the monsoon season, heavy rains can trigger landslides in mountainous regions like Himachal Pradesh and Uttarakhand. Check road conditions before traveling.

Typical Period: July - September
